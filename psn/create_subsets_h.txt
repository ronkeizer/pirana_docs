
  create_subsets

      The create_subsets script creates subsets of the individuals of
      a data set.

    Usage:

      create_subsets
      -bins=3
      [-idcolumn=column_number]
      [-stratify_on=column_number]
      datafile

    Example:

      ./create_subsets -idc=1 -bins=3 -stratify_on=5 datafile
